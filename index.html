  • Delft Dashboard

  • Welcome to the Delft Reactive Dashboard. This program is a reactive and interactive approach at communicating the information contained in the Delft 2040 impact analysis report, while visualising data about delft in an interactive way.

    Delft is looking to expand the city by adding 15,000 homes and 10,000 jobs by 2040. The impact report seeks to explore how those expansions will impact the city. This program uses the 6 categories established in the impact analysis. These categories and their respective scores can be seen below, and throughout the program.

    To explore these impacts, the program offers the functionality to increase the number of houses and jobs in the city, up to 15,000 and 10,000 respectively. The data visualisations will change to reflect these changes, and give warnings when problems occur, such as too much housing leading to a drop in mobility
